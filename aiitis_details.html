AIITIS

Overview

AIITIS is your gateway to a better mobility experience. AIITIS is designed to secure you during your rides.

Scan the driver's qr code to confrim and validate your driver's information. Enter your destination and confirm that you are on a trip. Your current location is shown on the map and an estimate of how long it should take you to reach your final destination. If you ever feel unsafe, please use the panic button to go into panic mode so we are aware of your location and can send someone to you.

Problem

Identifying a safe means of transportation is a guess game in Nigeria. Some days you will get it right, some days you will get it wrong. We would hate for you to lose your life or be plunged into financial ruin (in the case of a ransom).

Solution

We will monitor you during your trips. We will notify your emergency contacts in a panic situation. All our drivers go through a screening process before being onboarded onto this system. We will remind you to end your trips and after five reminders, we will alert your emergency contacts.

Scan the driver's qr code and make sure the information on the app matches the taxi information before entering the vechicle. Ensure that you confirm your trip on the app at the start of your trip. Ensure that you only use the panic button in a valid situation. Endeavour to end your trip at the end of your journey so we can confirm that you got to your destination safely.
