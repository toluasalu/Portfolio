Core

Overview

Core is a microfinance application designed to ease the process of acquiring and repaying microfinance loans. The mobile application is designed to be used by micrfinace institutiona agents who help their clients in creating savings and loan accounts, making loan repayments, desposits and withdrawals

An agent will do client registration based on the information provided by the client. This registration has to be approved by the admin, take loans and then savings and loan accounts can be setup for clients. Agents can make withdrawals, deposits, loan repayments on behalf of their clients using the Core Mobile App

Problem

There are certain financial habits that you have. You buy airtime/data, pay bills, borrow money, keep money away. Vuvaa is designed to ease doing this processes.

Solution

We facilitate faster and cheaper transfers. We help you get access to loans. We place everything you want to do at your fingertips.

We have the Airtime, Data service which allows you purchase airtime or data at no extra cost. TV Bills allows you to pay for cable tv like DSTV, GOTV, StarTimes e.tc. You can make transfers to other vuvaa accounts at no cost or to other bank accounts at a cost of N10. You can make multiple transfers at once using our bulk transfer service to both vuvaa and non-vuvaa accounts. Our Vault service allows you to safely lock money away. Our Loan Service gives you access to payday loans.
